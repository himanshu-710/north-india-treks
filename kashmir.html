Jammu & Kashmir Treks

Mesmerizing trails through paradise on Earth

Paradise for Trekkers

Jammu & Kashmir, often described as "Paradise on Earth," offers some of the most breathtaking trekking experiences in the world. From crystal-clear alpine lakes to vast meadows (locally known as "margs"), dense forests, and towering peaks, the region's diverse landscapes create a trekker's paradise.

Best Time to Visit

Spring (April-June)

A beautiful time as the snow begins to melt and wildflowers start blooming. Lower altitude treks are accessible, though higher passes may still be snow-covered.

Summer (July-September)

The prime trekking season in Kashmir. Alpine lakes thaw completely, meadows are lush green, and most high-altitude passes become accessible. Weather is generally stable.

Autumn (October-November)

A beautiful time with clear skies and autumn colors. Temperatures begin to drop, and higher altitude treks may see early snowfall by late October.

Winter (December-March)

Most high-altitude treks are inaccessible due to heavy snowfall. However, this is the perfect time for snow treks near Gulmarg and Pahalgam.
